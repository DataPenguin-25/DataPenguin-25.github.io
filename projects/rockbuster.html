Rockbuster Stealth Video Rental Analysis

Role: Data Analyst Tools: PostgreSQL, Tableau, Excel
View Tableau Dashboard → View GitHub Repository →

Project Overview

Rockbuster Stealth, a video rental company with a global presence, sought to transition from physical stores to an online streaming model. The management team required data-driven insights to understand customer distribution, revenue performance, and market opportunities across their international operations.

The Challenge

The business intelligence team posed several key questions:

  1. Which countries generate the highest revenue?
  2. What genres perform best across different markets?
  3. Where are high-value customers concentrated?
  4. How should the company prioritise its streaming launch strategy?

Data and Methodology

Database Exploration

I connected to Rockbuster's PostgreSQL database and explored fifteen related tables containing customer, rental, payment, inventory, and geographic data. I created an entity-relationship diagram and comprehensive data dictionary to document the database structure.

Analytical Approach

  • Revenue Analysis: Used SQL joins and aggregations to calculate total revenue by country and genre
  • Customer Analysis: Identified high-value customers and examined their geographic distribution
  • Market Segmentation: Ranked countries and cities by customer count and revenue contribution

Key Findings

Top Revenue Markets

The United States, India, and China generated the highest total revenue, representing the company's most valuable markets for the streaming transition.

Genre Performance

Sports, Science Fiction, and Animation produced the most consistent earnings across all regions, suggesting these genres should anchor the streaming catalogue.

Customer Distribution

High-value customers concentrated in the United States, Brazil, and the Netherlands. India and China led in total customer count, indicating strong growth potential.

Recommendations

  1. Maintain strong presence in the United States, India, and China to sustain revenue
  2. Expand streaming marketing efforts in emerging regions such as Brazil and Turkey
  3. Offer loyalty programmes targeting high-value customers to ensure retention
  4. Prioritise investments in top-performing genres (Sports, Science Fiction, Animation)

Reflections

This project strengthened my SQL proficiency, particularly in writing complex joins across multiple tables. The experience of exploring a relational database and documenting its structure prepared me well for enterprise data environments. Additionally, translating database findings into strategic recommendations reinforced my ability to bridge technical analysis with business communication.
