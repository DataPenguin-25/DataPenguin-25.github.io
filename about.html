About Me

Data Analyst

I'm a data analyst with a Bachelor of Business Administration from the University of Wisconsin-Whitewater and hands-on training in SQL, Python, and Tableau through CareerFoundry's Data Analytics Program. I'm drawn to work that turns messy data into clear insights that help people make better decisions.

My Background

After graduating with my BBA in 2023, I spent two years in Warsaw, Poland, teaching business English at American Language School. I worked with corporate clients from Poland, Ukraine, and Russia, helping professionals improve their communication skills in international business settings.

That experience taught me how to explain complex ideas clearly, adapt my approach to different audiences, and build relationships across cultural and language barriers. These skills translate directly to the work of a data analyst, where communicating findings to non-technical stakeholders is just as important as the analysis itself.

My Analytics Training

My BBA focused on Business Analytics, with coursework in Financial Analysis, Operations Management, Statistics, and Project Management. I'm currently completing CareerFoundry's Data Analytics Program, where I've built hands-on experience with SQL, Python, Tableau, and Excel through real-world projects.

I also hold CompTIA Tech+ certification, which gave me a solid foundation in systems, networking, and technical troubleshooting.

What I Bring

I combine technical analytics skills with strong communication abilities. I'm comfortable querying databases, building visualizations, and running statistical analysis. I'm equally comfortable presenting findings to people who don't speak SQL.

  • Stakeholder Communication

Looking Ahead
